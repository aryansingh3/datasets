Q: To have the car for the weekend, Wilson's report card needs to show that he received 80 or higher in his math class.  His math grade is calculated by the average of 5 test scores.  On those tests, he received: 65, 94, 81, 86 and 74.  What is his current math grade?
A: Let's think step by step. 

To have the car for the weekend, Wilson's report card needs to show that he received 80 or higher in his math class.

His math grade is calculated by the average of 5 test scores.

On those tests, he received: 65, 94, 81, 86 and 74.

To calculate the average, we need to add up all 5 numbers and then divide by 5. 

65 + 94 + 81 + 86 + 74 = 490

490 / 5 = 98

Wilson's current math grade is 98.
